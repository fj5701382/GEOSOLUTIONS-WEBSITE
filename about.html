Who We Are

Geosolution Educational Services is a premier edu-tech institution in Lagos, Nigeria, dedicated to fostering academic brilliance and equipping students with modern technological skills.

Established
October 22, 2012

Our Journey

Founded on October 22, 2012, Geosolution began with a simple belief: every student has the potential to excel given the right environment and guidance. Over the past decade, we have evolved from a traditional tutoring center into a comprehensive educational and technological hub.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, high-quality academic and technological training that equips students with the knowledge, skills, and confidence to succeed in competitive examinations and thrive in the modern digital economy.

Our Vision

To be the foremost educational institution in Nigeria recognized for producing academic high-achievers and tech-savvy innovators who positively impact society.
