Our Story

From Humble Beginnings to Over 1,000 Students

Geosolution Educational Services commenced operations on October 22, 2012, with a strong commitment to academic excellence and human capital development. Founded by passionate educators who identified the gap in quality, affordable exam preparation in Lagos, we set out to change the narrative for Nigerian students.

Over the years, we have expanded our services from simple JAMB coaching to a full suite of educational offerings including WAEC/NECO preparation, IELTS coaching, university admission processing, and our flagship GeoTech Academy for digital skills training.

Today, we operate from our centre at 12 Abayomi Street, Ikorodu Road, Lagos, serving hundreds of students annually. With a 95% exam pass rate and over 300 tech graduates, we remain unwavering in our commitment to transforming lives through education.
