NSCC: National Schools Scrabble and Chess Competition

Education goes beyond textbooks. The NSCC program is built to develop cognitive abilities, patience, and strategic foresight in students through competitive board games.

JOT: JAMB Orientation Training

A highly intensive preparatory program designed specifically for university aspirants. JOT demystifies the CBT exam format and equips students with the exact strategies needed to score 280+.
