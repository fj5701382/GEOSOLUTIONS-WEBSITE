Academic Programs

Exam Preparation & Academic Services

Expert coaching with proven results across all major Nigerian examinations.

JAMB / UTME Preparation

Intensive coaching for the Unified Tertiary Matriculation Examination with computer-based practice, past questions, and subject mastery sessions.

  • All UTME subjects covered
  • Daily mock CBT practice tests
  • Score prediction & strategy sessions
  • Results tracking & feedback

WAEC / NECO / GCE

Comprehensive preparation for WAEC, NECO and GCE examinations covering all science, art and commercial subjects.

  • Full syllabus coverage
  • Past question mastery
  • Practical sessions for science subjects
  • Essay and objective techniques

CBT Training

Hands-on computer-based test training designed to eliminate exam anxiety and maximise performance in all computer-administered exams.

  • Real exam simulation software
  • Speed & accuracy training
  • Keyboard and navigation skills
  • Time management strategies

Admission Processing

End-to-end support for university, polytechnic, and college of education admission processes — from JAMB to departmental screening.

  • JAMB change of institution
  • O'Level result upload & verification
  • Direct entry applications
  • Post-UTME preparation

IELTS / TOEFL Training

Structured IELTS and TOEFL preparation for students targeting international universities in the UK, Canada, USA, Australia, and beyond.

  • Listening, Reading, Writing, Speaking
  • Band-targeted coaching
  • Mock full-length test sessions
  • British Council exam registration guidance

Educational Consultancy

Expert guidance on academic pathways, career counselling, scholarship applications, and study-abroad strategies tailored to each student.

  • Career path counselling
  • Scholarship research & applications
  • Study abroad guidance
  • CV & personal statement writing
